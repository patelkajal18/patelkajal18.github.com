Game of Tree

- there are two players

- the player whose turn it is picks a card

- the card's value is added to your score unless it's a 1

- every turn you can choose to play or pass to the other player

- first player to 30 points wins and grows their tree

- oh, and if you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out
